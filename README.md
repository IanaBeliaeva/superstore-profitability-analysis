# superstore-profitability-analysis

**Tools:** SQL, Power BI  
**Dataset:** Sample Superstore  
**Author:** Iana Beliaeva

[View the full project report (PDF)](Superstore_Profitability_Analysis.pdf)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes sales, profit margins, discount patterns, and regional performance to identify profitability risks and focus areas for management.

## Business Questions
- Which categories and sub-categories create profitability risks?
- How are discount levels associated with profit margin?
- What drives low profitability in the Central region?

## Key Findings
- Superstore generated a 12.47% overall profit margin.
- Furniture had the lowest category profit margin at 2.49%.
- Tables and Bookcases were loss-making Furniture sub-categories.
- In the observed data, sales at discount levels of 30% or higher had a negative aggregate profit margin.
- In the Central region, Furniture was the only loss-making category.

## Recommended Next Steps
- Review discount policy for Furniture, particularly Tables.
- Investigate pricing and cost structure for loss-making Furniture in Central.
- Monitor profit margin by region, category, sub-category, and discount level.

## Note
This analysis identifies relationships in historical sales data. Further validation would require product-level cost and order-volume data.
